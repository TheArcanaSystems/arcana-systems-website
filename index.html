Meet the Founder

"I build systems so you can build your business."

The architecture of trust: meet the founder behind The Arcana Method.

The systems you need. The trust you deserve.

Every founder I work with has the same story: the business grew faster than the operations could handle. The tools multiplied. The processes stayed in someone's head. And somewhere along the way, they realized they'd become the bottleneck.

I know that story because I've lived versions of it: on the client side, the consulting side, and now on the building side.

Before Arcana Systems, I spent years working with boutique agencies and professional service firms as a strategist, systems lead, and operational partner. I watched brilliant founders burn 20 hours a week on tasks that should have been handled by a system. I saw teams with world-class talent held back by duct-taped workflows. And I realized that most "operations solutions" fell into two camps: too hands-off (templates you have to build yourself) or too heavy (expensive consultants who hand you a report and disappear).

There had to be a better way.

The Arcana Systems was built to fill that gap. We don't hand you templates and wish you luck. We don't spend months diagnosing problems without building solutions. We do something simpler and harder: we build your operating system from Audit to Blueprint to Build to Steward, and we stay with you until it runs without you.

I lead every engagement personally, from the first Arcana Audit to the final handover. My role is to understand your business deeply enough to design systems that feel like they were built for you. Because they were.

That's the promise I make to every client: not a system that works. A system that works for you.
