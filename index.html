About Caitilin

Systems create freedom. Freedom creates impact.

For more than 20 years, Caitilin Nelson has worked across finance, talent acquisition, and operations, finding the points where manual processes quietly drain time, create risk, and keep founders trapped as the bottleneck.

Arcana Systems exists for solopreneurs and small teams with brilliant work, scattered tools, and no reliable operating system. We build the architecture behind the vision: CRM structure, automations, documentation, client portals, and clean handoffs.
